  - subject:     release-evidence/v2.9.34/witness-v2.9.34.json
  - SHA-256:     5a6abd78fac05bde9951b17f9ca27d2fd163b85ef956df18ced84cc214cc1f78

The Rekor log is append-only and not under the project maintainer's control — opposing
counsel can query Rekor directly and verify the entry exists, was signed at the
documented timestamp, and points to this repository under the GitHub OIDC issuer.
