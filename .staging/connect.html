Before You Send

What helps us scope a forensic engagement.

  • Project type and sector — e.g. municipal infrastructure, nuclear refurbishment, commercial fit-out, mining
  • Current dispute status — notice phase, EOT submitted, mediation, arbitration, or litigation
  • The XER file(s) — baseline plus updates if you have them; we’ll work with what you can share
  • Correspondence record — RFIs, change directives, design responses, access logs, where available
  • Target turnaround — preliminary assessment in two weeks is the standard short scope; full forensic analysis runs four to ten weeks

NDA on request — we sign standard mutual NDAs before any documents move. None of the above is required to start a conversation; it just shortens the back-and-forth on scoping.
